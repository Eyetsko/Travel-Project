ABOUT ME

EMILY YETSKO

Since I studied abroad in 2014 I have been to 26 countries, on 4 continents. I have done the majority of my traveling alone which gives me a lot of reason to want to capture everything I see. All of my photos are taken on a cellphone, various models throughout the years.
I am particulary drawn to scenery and architecture. I was blown away by the greens of Ireland, and the crystal blue oceans in Puerto Rico.
Italy holds a special place in my heart. I studied in Florence and fell in love with the food, the language, and the beauty. I have been to more than 20 Italian cities over the years. I hope to make one of them my home someday soon.
